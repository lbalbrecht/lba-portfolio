About Me

I am a web developer based in Seattle, Washington, who is interested in moving into software development. My interests are in artificial intelligence, augmented reality, virtual reality, and video games.

I'm intrigued by what separates an organic brain from an artificial mind and how we can utilize our understanding of human neurology in the development of video game AI, immersive virtual environments, and haptic feedback. I'm also interested in the ways in which we can utilize machine learning in medicine and healthcare and if it is possible to emulate neurodegeneration in artificial intelligence to further our understanding of how to treat these conditions.

In 2021, I received a certificate of completion of Trilogy Education's Coding Boot Camp from the University of Washington in Seattle, WA specializing in full-stack web development. In 2018, I received my BA in Cognitive Behavioral Neuroscience from The College of Wooster in Wooster, OH.
